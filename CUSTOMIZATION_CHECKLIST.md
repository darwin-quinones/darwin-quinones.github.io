## ✨ Pro Tips

1. **Professional Photos**: Invest in professional headshots if possible
2. **Project Images**: Use high-quality screenshots or mockups
3. **Writing**: Keep descriptions concise and impact-focused
4. **Metrics**: Include numbers when possible (e.g., "Improved performance by 40%")
5. **Keywords**: Include relevant keywords for SEO
6. **Mobile First**: Most recruiters will view on mobile, test thoroughly
7. **Loading Speed**: Compress images to keep site fast
8. **Regular Updates**: Keep portfolio current with latest work
